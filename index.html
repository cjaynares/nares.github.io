BACKGROUND ON INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

The foundation of today's digital society is information technology (IT), which includes a wide range of devices, programs, and procedures that make it easier to create, save, retrieve, and share data and information. Since its beginnings in the middle of the 20th century, when computers first became available, information technology (IT) has developed into a vital field that supports almost every facet of contemporary life. IT experts are essential to promoting innovation, efficiency, and connectedness across industries, from the creation of intricate software applications to the administration of international networks. Artificial intelligence, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and the Internet of Things (IoT) are just a few of the new frontiers that IT has entered because to the lightning-fast speed of technological innovation, presenting both uncommon potential and difficulties.
